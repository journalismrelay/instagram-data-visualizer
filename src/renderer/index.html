How to get your Instagram data

1

Go to accountscenter.instagram.com

2

Click Your information and permissions

3

Click Export your information → Create export

4

Select your Instagram profile → Export to device

5

Set: Information All data · Date range All time · Format JSON preferred (HTML also works) · Media quality Any

6

Submit and wait — Instagram will notify you when ready (up to 48h)

7

Download the file and unzip it

8

Click the button above and select the unzipped folder

9

There is nothing else!
